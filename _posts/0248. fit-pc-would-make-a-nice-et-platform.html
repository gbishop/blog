--- categories: Enabling Technology date: 2007/07/20 20:04:35 permalink: /~gb/blog/2007/07/20/fit-pc-would-make-a-nice-et-platform/ tags: '' title: fit-PC would make a nice ET platform ---

The fit-PC looks like a very interesting platform for ET projects. It is cheap ($285), small (4.7x4.6x1.6 inches), low power (3 to 5 watts) and very capable:

  • 500 MHz AMD Geode LX800 processor
  • 256 MB of DDR RAM
  • 40 GB hard drive
  • Dual 100 Mbps Ethernet
  • SXGA graphics controller, resolution to 1920 x 1440
  • 2 USB 2.0 high speed ports
  • Audio in/out interfaces
  • RS-232 serial port
  • Single 5V power supply
