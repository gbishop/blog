Make homemade walnut granola.

Mix together aproximately

  • 1/2 cup of honey,
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons of blackstrap molasses,
  • a tablespoon of vanilla,
  • a dash of salt, and
  • a teaspoon each of your favorite spices, such as cinnamon, ginger and/or nutmeg.

Place 6â€“8 cups of rolled oats in a large bowl and toss to coat with the honey-blackstrap mixture. Then spread on a cookie sheet and bake at 275 degrees for 45 minutes. Cool and mix in 1/2 to 1 cup of walnuts.
