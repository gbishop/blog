  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup blanched, natural California pistachios, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated orange peel
Heat half-and-half in saucepan; stir in sugar and salt. Pour a small amount of hot half-and-half into egg yolks, stirring constantly. Return yolk mixture to half-and-half; cook and stir over medium heat about 5 to 10 minutes or until thickened and creamy. Do not boil. Cool. Stir in vanilla and heavy cream. Chill. Pour into freezer container; follow manufacturerâ€™s directions for freezing. Add pistachios and orange peel when almost frozen; freeze until firm. Allow ice cream to stand at least 2 hours in refrigerator-freezer to mellow flavors.

Blanching Process:

Drop shelled nuts into boiling water, remove from heat and let them soak for about one minute. Drain and rub the pistachios with a clean kitchen towel. To dry, spread on a large baking sheet in an oven preheated to 300 degrees F. for 10 to 15 minutes.
